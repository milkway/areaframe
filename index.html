Area Frames

  1. Sampling units can assume a variety of forms
  2. Built upon GPS/GIS/Remote sensing type of data
  3. Furnishes complete population coverage
  4. Keeps updated over time
  5. Provides indirect access to reporting units
  6. Needs maintenance for stratification purposes
  7. High costs to build an area frame
  8. Finding reporting units can require high efforts
